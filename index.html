About

Matt works as a Game Designer, utilizing C#, C++, Java, and Swift. He has released titles on Xbox One, Playstation 4, Nintendo Switch, PC, iOS, Android, and Wii U. Matt has been part of both in-house and remote development teams, and has also worked on projects for clients such as Disney, Sony, and the NBA.

History

Graduating Western Kentucky University in 2013, Matt earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science. He has worked as a designer or developer since graduating, for nine years.

Expertise

Proficient with designing 3D levels in the Unity, Unreal Engine, and Creation Kit editors, Matt is also experienced with object-oriented programming.

Future plans

Enjoying keeping up-to-date with the latest games and design practices, Matt hopes to continually advance his game development and design skills.
